You Don't Have to Live in Pain

Surgical intervention is utilized as a last resort in cases where other treatments have failed. ACMH Hospital is home to sophisticated medical care for all your neurosurgical needs including, but not limited to:

  • Back pain
  • Neck pain
  • Vertebral fractures
  • Herniated disc
  • Spinal stenosis
  • Chiari malformation
  • Brain tumor
  • Carpal tunnel syndrome
  • Ulnar neuropathy
  • Meningioma
  • Schwanoma
  • Synovial cyst
  • Spondylolisthesis
  • Spinal cord stimulator implants
  • Intrathecal medication delivery pumps
  • Scoliosis
  • Subdural hematomas
  • Normal pressure hydrocephalus
  • Foot drop
