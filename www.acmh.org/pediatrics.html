Through a partnership with Children's Community Pediatrics (CCP), ACMH now provides your child with access to specialized pediatric care closer to home.

Children's Community Pediatrics

As the first pediatric practice in Armstrong County, CCP – Armstrong has been providing pediatric care to children and teens throughout the region since 1958. With two office locations – one in Kittanning and one in Sarver – CCP is available when your family needs us most.

Through a partnership with ACMH, you can now see CCP – Armstrong pediatricians at two ACMH Primary Care Centers in Elderton and Leechburg.

As a part of the Children’s Community Pediatrics network, and under the leadership and guiding principles of UPMC Children’s Hospital of Pittsburgh, CCP – Armstrong is dedicated to offering exceptional pediatric care, right in your community.
