Michelle Keibler is an acute care nurse practitioner, state-certified through the American Nurses Credentialing Center. She has worked the last twelve years for UPMC Pulmonary Consultants as the Senior Advanced Practice Provider, rounding at UPMC St. Margaret and ACMH inpatient services, as well as outpatient settings. She also works part time for the ACMH Hospitalist service. Keibler spearheaded the ACMH Grand Rounds continuing medical education program at ACMH, and precepts Nurse Practitioner students for Duquesne University, Pittsburgh. She earned her Bachelor of Science degree in Nursing at IUP. Also, Keibler had scholarships during her Masters program, and presented at research events at Cleveland Clinic and UPMC St. Margaret.
