Urology

ACMH urology services encompass a wide variety of specialized care for diseases of the male and female urinary tract and reproductive organs.
  1. Home
  2. Urology

Armstrong Urology Associates is equipped with the latest medical knowledge, diagnostic technology, and surgical equipment to provide patients with specialized, innovative treatment.

Advanced Services

As a healthcare provider who focuses on taking the lead in new technology, Dr. Jatinder Kumar also strives to offer the latest treatments in urology care. Dr. Kumar’s mission is to improve quality of life by decreasing the burden of urologic disease or life-changing conditions through excellent clinical care.

Armstrong Urology Associates recently welcomed urologist Muhammad Umar Alam, MD to the practice. Dr. Alam joins Jatinder Kumar M.D., M.Ch, Chief, Department of Urology, and is looking forward to welcoming new patients. Call 724-548-3884 to schedule and appointment.

  • Clinical Specialties
  • Dr. Kumar's Achievements

  • Robotic surgeries for urologic cancer
  • Enlarged prostate treatment (including UroLift and HoLEP)
  • Prostate cancer, including robotic radical prostatectomy (SpaceOAR Hydrogel)
  • Laser procedures for kidney stones
  • Urinary incontinence (including InterStim and AUS)
  • Penile implants for ED after prostate cancer treatment
  • Percutaneous nephrolithotomy (PCNL) and mini-PCNL for kidney stones
  • Reconstructive urology including complex urethroplasties
  • Uro-oncology services
  • Pediatric urology

  • Over 10 years of urology experience
  • 8,000+ urology procedures performed
  • Experience in robotic surgeries for urologic cancers and reconstruction
  • Developed holmium laser enucleation of prostate (HOLEP) program at the University of Florida
  • Developed mini-percutaneous nephrolithotomy (mini-PCNL) program at the University of Florida
  • Created advanced urology center at Anand Hospital, India; used as referral center and trained urologists in reconstructive surgery
  • Pioneered laparoscopic adult and pediatric urology at Anand Hospital
