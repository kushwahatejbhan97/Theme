Results You Can Trust

The ACMH Hospital Laboratory is a full service lab offering a broad range of tests with technically proven methodologies for diagnostic services. The professional staff of pathology physicians, technologists, phlebotomists and support staff are highly trained and dedicated to meeting all patient needs. Specialized consulting services are provided by board-certified pathologists.

The lab is located on the first floor of ACMH Hospital, to the right of the lobby behind the Gift Shop.

FAQ

Do I need to fast before my test?
The most common tests that require a 12-hour fast are glucose (blood sugar) and cholesterol (lipid panel). The lab order often states when fasting is required.

Do I need an appointment?
Most lab tests do not need an appointment.

How long will it be until my doctor receives the test results?
Most routine tests are complete and results delivered to physician offices require 24 hours. Specialized testing that is not performed at ACMH may require 3-5 days. Lab results can be accessed via the Patient Portal.

Services

The ACMH Hospital Laboratory benefits health care providers and their patients by:

  • Providing convenient local access to STAT and routine testing
  • Implementing clinically relevant test strategies
  • Offering personalized customer service with consultative support from local pathologists and laboratory professionals
  • Ensuring continuity of care between inpatient and outpatient testing
  • Keeping local dollars in the community

Specialized Services:

  • Consulting Services provided by board-certified pathologists.
  • Diagnostic Services – full service laboratory offering a broad range of tests with technically proven methodologies
    • Automated Report delivery
  • Anatomic Pathology including advanced technology in Pap Smear screening
  • Microbiology – Includes parasite identification, bacterial, fungal and viral disease detection
  • Transfusion Medicine – Access to regional blood centers
  • Outreach Services – Nine conveniently located lab collection sites throughout the county

The ACMH Hospital Laboratory has the following accreditations:

• College of American Pathologists (CAP)
• Food and Drug Administration (FDA) for Transfusion Medicine
• Clinical Laboratory Improvement Amendments (CLIA) 
• Pennsylvania Department of Health
