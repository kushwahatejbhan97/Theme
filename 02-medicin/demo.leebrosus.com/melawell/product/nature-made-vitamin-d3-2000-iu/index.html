Description

Maldox 500/25mg Tablets 3 Tablets

This is a combination of sulfadoxine, a sulfa medicine, and pyrimethamine combination is used to treat malaria. Pyrimethamine and sulfadoxine may also be used to prevent malaria in people who are living in or will be traveling to, an area where there is a chance of getting malaria.

Side Effect:
  • Consult your doctor if you observe any of the following side-effects, especially if they do not go away.
  • Inflammation of the tongue
  • Abdominal pain
  • Urinary retention
  • Headache
  • Fatigue
  • Painful urination
  • Nosebleed
  • Feeling of discomfort
  • Urge to vomit
